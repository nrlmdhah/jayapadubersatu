Company Introduction

JAYAPADU BERSATU KAWALAN SDN. BHD [481560-A]

Jayapadu Bersatu Kawalan Sdn Bhd is a fully-fledged commercial and industrial security agency registered in Malaysia under the Private Agency Act 1971. We provide a wide range of professional security services to meet the growing demand for trusted and reliable security solutions across the country.

Our Vision

To be the leading security company in Malaysia by supporting national development and aligning with the “Vision of Shared Prosperity 2030”.

Our Mission

As a business-oriented security agency, our core activity is delivering professional security services. Our purpose is to safeguard people, protect property, and serve the nation with integrity and dedication.

Unity in protection, Excellence in Service.

Our Company Objective

Jayapadu Bersatu Kawalan Sdn Bhd provides security services for local and foreign industries throughout Malaysia. We emphasize delivering reliable, high-value services aligned with the region’s industrial growth and globalization.

We aim to carry out specialized security services and integrate state-of-the-art technology with human security. Our operations focus on the highest quality of management, strong financial control, and strategic task alignment.

Leveraging the experience and resourcefulness of our personnel, we solve challenges efficiently and professionally.

In line with Malaysia's Vision of Shared Prosperity 2030, we emphasize Human Resource Training & Development to seize new industrial opportunities.

Jayapadu Bersatu Kawalan Sdn Bhd continues to innovate, expand, and offer its expertise to a growing range of industries and clients.
