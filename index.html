Occupational Safety and Health

Jayapadu Bersatu Kawalan Sdn Bhd prioritizes the safety and health of its employees as a key management responsibility.

Top Management Priority

Safety and health are treated as a top-level responsibility across all levels of the company.

Clear Objectives

Each department sets and works towards specific safety and health targets.

Safe Operations & Maintenance

All operations and maintenance include safety considerations as part of the process.

Integrated Design

Safety is built into the design, construction, and operation of all facilities.

Employee Responsibility

Each employee is responsible for safety, supported by regular communication and training.

Continuous Audit & Improvement

Regular audits focus on hygiene, accident prevention, and improving workplace safety.

Unity in protection, Excellence in Service.

Managing Environmental Performance
